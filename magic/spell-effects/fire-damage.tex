\subsection*{Fire Damage}
\unclassedprop{School}{Pyromancy} \\
\unclassedprop{Targeting}{Corporeal}\\
\unclassedprop{Duration}{3 rounds/Instantaneous}\\
\unclassedprop{Mana Cost}{Spellcrafting dice cost \texttimes ½}

Choose spellcrafting dice for this spell.

Deals fire damage equal to the dice roll to target(s) each round for the
duration, starting from the moment this spell hits.

If you choose instantaneous duration, the mana cost is as if it was 1
round.
