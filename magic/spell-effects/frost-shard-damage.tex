\subsection*{Frost Shard Damage}
\unclassedprop{School}{Cryomancy} \\
\unclassedprop{Targeting}{Corporeal}\\
\unclassedprop{Duration}{1 round/Instantaneous}\\
\unclassedprop{Mana Cost}{Spellcrafting dice cost \texttimes ⅛}

Choose spellcrafting dice for this spell.

Deals frost piercing ("and" relationship, so weaknesses and resistances
only need one of the types to apply) damage equal to the dice roll to
target(s) each round for the duration, starting from the moment this
spell hits.
