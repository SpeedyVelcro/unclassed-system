\subsection*{Armor}
\unclassedprop{School}{Warding} \\
\unclassedprop{Targeting}{Incorporeal} \\
\unclassedprop{Duration}{12 hours} \\
\unclassedprop{Mana Cost}{3 \texttimes X + (P + Q + R)\textsuperscript{2}}

The target gains an additional layer of weightless armor with cover
rating X and an armor rating of P bludgeoning, Q piercing, and R
slashing. An unwilling creature may resist the effects of this spell
using a WIL (discipline) check.
