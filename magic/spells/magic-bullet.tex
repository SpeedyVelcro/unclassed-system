\subsection*{Magic Bullet}
\unclassedprop{school}{Aeromancy} \\
\unclassedprop{Gestures}{Vocal, Two Hands} \\
\unclassedprop{Casting Time}{1 action} \\
\unclassedprop{Duration}{Instantaneous} \\
\unclassedprop{Target}{Ranged} \\
\unclassedprop{Range}{30 ft.} \\
\unclassedprop{Damage}{2d12 bludgeoning} \\
\unclassedprop{MP Cost}{5} \\
\unclassedprop{Spell Effects}{Force Damage}

On casting this spell, the caster hurls a magical projectile in an
arc towards the target. The caster makes a ranged magic attack against
one creature that deals 2d12 bludgeoning damage on hit.
