\subsection*{Ice Spike}
\unclassedprop{School}{Cryomancy} \\
\unclassedprop{Gestures}{Vocal, Two Hands} \\
\unclassedprop{Casting Time}{1 Action} \\
\unclassedprop{Duration}{Instantaneous} \\
\unclassedprop{Target}{Melee} \\
\unclassedprop{Damage}{3d4 frost piercing damage} \\
\unclassedprop{MP Cost}{3} \\
\unclassedprop{Spell Effects}{Frost Shard Damage}

On casting this spell, the caster thrusts their palm towards an enemy,
out of which protudes a razor-sharp shard of ice. The caster makes a
melee magic attack against one creature that deals 3d4 frost piercing
damage.

Note that the damage type has an "and" relationship by default, meaning
the target only needs to have resistance/armor or weakness to one of
the damage types for the strongest applicable weakness or resistance to
apply.
