\subsection*{Shield}
\unclassedprop{School}{Warding} \\
\unclassedprop{Gestures}{Vocal, Two Hands} \\
\unclassedprop{Target}{Self} \\
\unclassedprop{Casting Time}{Reaction} \\
\unclassedprop{MP Cost}{1} \\
\unclassedprop{Spell Effects}{Shield}

The caster uses magical energy to block an incoming blow. Any time the
caster would be able to make a block skill check, the caster may
instead use their warding skill check in its place. The warding skill
check is treated as if it was a block skill check.
