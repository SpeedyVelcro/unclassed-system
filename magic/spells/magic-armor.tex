\subsection*{Magic Armor}
\unclassedprop{School}{Warding} \\
\unclassedprop{Gestures}{Vocal, Two Hands} \\
\unclassedprop{Target}{Self} \\
\unclassedprop{Casting Time}{1 minute} \\
\unclassedprop{Duration}{8 hours}
\unclassedprop{MP Cost}{4} \\
\unclassedprop{Spell Effects}{Armor}

The caster creates a long-lasting ward around their body. Upon
successful casting of this spell, the caster gains an additional layer
armor. This armor is ethereal and weightless, and has a cover rating of
6 and an armor rating of 2b/2p/2s
